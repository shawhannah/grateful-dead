how to play

At the start of the game, you'll be assigned a random number. Below your number are four bears. Each bear has a random value. Each time you click a bear, that value is added to your total score.

Try to get your score as close to the random number as you can without going over. If you match your score to the random number, you're the Bear Bonanza Champion!
